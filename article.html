The Modern Application of Stoic Resilience

By Dr. Elena Rostova • Oct 12, 2025 • 12 min read

In an era defined by constant digital connectivity and unparalleled global uncertainty, the ancient Greco-Roman philosophy of Stoicism is experiencing a dramatic renaissance. But how do we apply the teachings of an emperor and a slave to the anxieties of the 21st century?

The Dichotomy of Control

Epictetus famously stated, "Some things are in our control and others not." This fundamental tenet serves as the bedrock for modern cognitive behavioral therapy. Today, as we are bombarded by 24-hour news cycles concerning events thousands of miles away, recognizing what falls within our sphere of influence is critical for mental homeostasis.

"You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength." — Marcus Aurelius
